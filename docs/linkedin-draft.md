## About

I've been engineering software for 20 years. These days I design infrastructure and architect solutions — I don't write code anymore, AI does that. My job is knowing what to build, why, and how to get there.

This isn't about replacing engineers with AI. It's about a different way of working: I bring 20 years of pattern recognition, architectural judgment, and knowing how systems behave in production. AI brings implementation speed and breadth of knowledge. We work as equals — I design, push back, ask hard questions. AI implements, proposes alternatives, catches things I'd miss.

What experience gives you isn't memorized APIs — I couldn't write an Elasticsearch query from memory even though I built production infrastructure on it. What it gives you is recognizing when a shortcut will break in six months, knowing the right follow-up question because you've seen how systems fail, and understanding that there is no instant software. It's a growing, changing thing that takes time to understand what you actually want.

Through my company Metaintellect, I've been collaborating with A+E Global Media for 5 years as an external Principal Lead — architecting Pulumi infrastructure components, building monitoring systems, and teaching the team how to work effectively with AI. On my own time, I built a RAG system for Croatian legal documents and a full-stack React app — both entirely with AI, neither of which I could have built as fast or as broadly without it.

Based in Rijeka, Croatia. Available for contract work and technical collaboration.
